About Me

Parker Greenwell grew up in West Weber, Utah. He graduated Brigham Young University with a degree in Physics Education. He went on to teach Physics and Environmental Science at Mountain View High School in Orem, UT. In the fall of 2022 he began a Full-Stack Bootcamp, and is becoming proficient with html, css, and Javascript. He currently lives in Orem with his wife and his daughter.
